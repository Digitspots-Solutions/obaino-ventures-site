About Us

Obaino Ventures is a leading manufacturing company based in Wamakko, Sokoto State. Operating from our facility at Arkilla Federal Low-cost Sokoto, we are dedicated to providing top-tier manufacturing solutions that meet the needs of Nigerian businesses and industries. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and timely delivery has positioned us as a reliable partner for companies seeking exceptional manufacturing services. We combine modern technology with skilled expertise to deliver products that exceed industry standards.
